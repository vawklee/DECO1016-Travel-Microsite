Ferry Services

You have to travel by ferry to get to Cheung Chau, and the only ferry service departs from the Central station Pier #5 approximately every half an hour (Central is accessible via the Mass Transit Railway service across Hong Kong).

There is a slow and fast ferry service, with fares depending on the service you choose to take. Ferry services begin to depart from Hong Kong at 1:30am, with the ending service being at 12:30am. Services departing from Cheung Chau begin at 2:20am and end at 11:45pm, with the trip duration varying between 30 to 60 minutes depending on the service.

Cheung Chau | 長洲

Cheung Chau is an island, in the shape of a dumbbell, south-west off the coast of Hong Kong. This island features plenty of beautiful natural scenery and coastal sites worth visiting. There are various hiking trails, temples and markets to explore, as well as lots of local cuisine to taste.

If you're looking for a weekend getaway or a day trip where you get to relax and just appreciate the island environment around you, Cheung Chau is the place for you!
